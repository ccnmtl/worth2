The study ID consists of 13 digits: RRHHMMDDMMYBS where the letters correspond to the following:

  • RR - Research Assistant
  • HH - hour (24-hour clock)
  • MM - minute
  • DD - day
  • MM - month
  • Y - last digit of year (5, 6, or 7)
  • B - borough (Bronx=1, Brooklyn=2, Manhattan=3, Queens=4, Staten Island=5)
  • S - Fortune site (LIC=1, Harlem=2)
