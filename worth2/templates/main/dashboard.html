E-WORTH

Empowering African-American Women on the Road to Health

Welcome to E-WORTH! As part of this course you will find information and resources to help you protect your own health, the health of your family, and of your community. You can move through the sections at your own pace and you don’t have to complete it all at once – you can stop and come back. While we recommend that you move through the sections in order you can skip ahead.

We do ask that you complete E-WORTH within 90 days. Accounts and associated data are deleted at that point to ensure your privacy and the security of your data. If you wish to return to E-WORTH after 90 days have passed you may sign up again.

Click "Get Started" to meet Carol, who will help guide you through the course, activities and scenarios.
